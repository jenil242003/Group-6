About Income Prediction Form

This is an income prediction form. You need to select your age, then choose your working class. Next, select your education level followed by your marital status. After that, indicate your occupation, relationship status, race, and gender. Then input your capital gain and capital loss, followed by the number of hours you work per week. Finally, select your native country. Upon hitting submit, the form will predict whether your income is more than or less than $50,000.
